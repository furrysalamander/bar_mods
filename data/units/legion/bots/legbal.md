## Description
The Ballista is a medium-range rocket bot built for sustained damage and fire support. With solid health and excellent range, it’s ideal for wearing down enemy frontlines or punishing static defenses from safety.

Though slow and vulnerable if caught alone, Ballistas shine when positioned behind tanks or facing into chokepoints. Their ranged pressure forces enemy movement and enables Legion to take favorable engagements.
