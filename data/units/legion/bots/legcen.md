## Description
The Phobos is a lightning-fast assault bot designed to overwhelm enemies with speed and brutal close-range firepower. Armed with a high-damage gauss cannon, it excels at ambushing fragile targets, chasing down retreating units, and diving into exposed backlines. Its combo of speed and health makes it closer to a light tank than an infantry bot.

While it can’t withstand prolonged engagements or heavy turret fire, its hit-and-run potential is unmatched at the T1 stage. Use Phobos squads to fracture enemy formations, disrupt artillery lines, or pounce on overextended constructors.
