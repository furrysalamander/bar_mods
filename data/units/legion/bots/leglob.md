## Description
The Satyr is a dependable light plasma bot that excels in large numbers. With solid range and power, it forms the backbone of Legion’s early ground forces, capable of trading well against most T1 threats.

Roughly two Satyrs can be fielded for every equivalent plasma bot, though with inferior stats. Spread them out to secure flanking damage bonuses and avoid AoE damage.
