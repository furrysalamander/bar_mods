## Description
The Bastion is a T2 defensive platform equipped with a sweeping beam weapon capable of sustained area denial. Though costly, it excels at punishing concentrated assaults and slow-pushing enemies. However, it lacks anti-air capabilities, and should be complemented by dedicated AA defenses.

It also features a "turtle shell" mode, toggled via its ON/OFF button, which transforms it into a fortified wall. While in this mode, its durability increases by 300%, making it exceptionally resistant to artillery and able to survive a direct hit from a nuke. This defensive stance is especially useful when under heavy long-range fire.
