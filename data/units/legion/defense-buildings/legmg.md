## Description
The Cacophony shreds light armor and air units alike with a continuous stream of high-velocity kinetic rounds. Its burst damage makes it a strong deterrent to fast skirmishers and air units alike.

Compared to other factions' heavy T1 defenses, the Cacophony has slightly less range and per-shot power, but has better short-range killing power and the ability to deal with air units. However, it's better vs. gunships than it is bombers and fighters.
