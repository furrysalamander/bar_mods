## Description
The Dragon's Jaw is a deceptive defense structure built to draw enemy fire and shield more valuable targets. Mimicking a section of Dragon's Teeth, it misleads opponents and ambushes them when they get close. Due to its stealth capabilities and reduced damage intake, it serves well as a frontline misdirection tool. However, its actual firepower and utility are limited, so it should always be paired with real threats.
