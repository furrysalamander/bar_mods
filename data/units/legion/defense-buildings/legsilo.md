## Description
The Supernova stockpiles and fires Intercontinental Ballistic Missiles (ICBMs), also known as nuclear missiles. They can cross the entire map to obliterate entire bases in a single shot.The Supernova stockpiles and fires Intercontinental Ballistic Missiles (ICBMs), also known as nuclear missiles. They can cross the entire map to obliterate entire bases in a single shot.

Each missile deals 11,500 damage in a 1,920 AoE. Commanders uniquely take only 2,500 from nukes, and can survive a blast if at full health. Each missile costs 1500 metal and 187,500 energy, and takes 3 minutes to stockpile.
