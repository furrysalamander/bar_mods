## Description
The Amputator unleashes cluster munitions that devastate wide areas, ideal for punishing slow-moving or massed enemy ground units. With both direct and high-arc trajectories, it adapts well to varied terrain and unit density.

While sharing an equivalent role to other factions' T1 static artillery, the Amputator is noticeably less expensive. Its sheer impact and area denial potential make it a centerpiece in entrenched defenses.
