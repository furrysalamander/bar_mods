## Description
The Rampart is a high-tech multipurpose geothermal powerplant. It generates energy, jams enemy radar, detects units over a wide range, and acts as both an anti-nuke interceptor and drone carrier. It automatically stockpiles interceptors and deploys heavy drones for battlefield support. This makes it a strategic cornerstone in late-game defense and area denial.
