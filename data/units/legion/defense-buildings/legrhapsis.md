## Description
The Rhapsis saturates the skies with a rapid salvo of guided AA munitions, ideal for overwhelming light and fast-moving aircraft. Its wide coverage and tracking make it a powerful counter to swarm air tactics.

Though it fires in bursts, each individual missile is relatively weak — so it's best used to chip away at enemy air formations.
