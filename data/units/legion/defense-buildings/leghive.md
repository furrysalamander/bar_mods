## Description
The Hive is an autonomous drone carrier that builds and maintains a squadron of 6 flying attack drones. The drones attack whatever the Hive targets. Drones are useful for cheaply gaining vision over target areas, harassing enemy forces, and chasing fast raiders.

Drones deal 36 damage at 300 range every 1.8s. Each has 415 health, and flies at 280.5 speed. They have 600 sight range. The Hive builds a new drone every 8s.
