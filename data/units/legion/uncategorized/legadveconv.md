## Description
Legion's Advanced Energy Converter can convert up to 600 energy into 10.3 metal per second֫—a better ratio than 10 basic T1 converters could achieve. They form an important keystone of late-game economic builds.

However, advanced converters are very fragile and prone to chain-exploding. Try not to leave them vulnerable to attack, and don't put them too close to other important infrastructure.
