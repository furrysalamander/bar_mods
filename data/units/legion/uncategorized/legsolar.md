## Description
The Solar Collector produces energy using the sun. Solar energy production is a constant +20 and is essential when wind speeds are low. They’re not as cost-effective as Wind Turbines, and instead, provide a safe and stable source of energy.

When attacked, Solar Collectors automatically close to reduce further damage taken. They do not produce energy while closed.
