## Description
The Daedalus is a tier-3 heavy railgun platform designed for long-range siege and direct firepower dominance. Its main cannon, the Siege Rail Accelerator, deals devastating armor-piercing shots ideal for shredding high-HP targets and breaking static defenses. Its railgun can even pierce through enemies and hit those standing behind!

In addition to its primary weapon, it features quad-mounted machine guns for close-range suppression and anti-light screening—an oddity among artillery units. With solid durability and surprising mobility for its size, it can push through defended zones and serve as the backbone of a late-game armored column.
