## Description
The Incinerator is a slow-moving weapon platform built around obliteration through overwhelming firepower. Equipped with a sustained heavy heat ray, it melts through even the most fortified ground targets with brutal efficiency. Despite its overwhelming firepower and solid durability, the Incinerator crawls across the battlefield at a glacial pace, making it easy to kite, flank, or disable if not properly escorted. It excels when anchoring a frontline or sieging hardened positions with supporting forces.

Its heat ray fires constantly, with no reload time—though it does cost considerable energy to maintain. It deals damage in 72 AoE around whatever it hits. Because it can only fire in a straight line, be sure to keep it on the front where it won't be blocked by your own forces.
