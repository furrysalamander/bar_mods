## Description
Essentially an upgunned Lance, the Arquebus' railgun deals roughly four times the damage at 200 more range. And like the Lance, its shots can overpenetrate to damage enemies behind the primary target. It pairs this with spider mobility, letting it scale cliffs to get shots from any angle. They are perfect for long-range pressure, destroying defenses, and sniping important units dead.

Like most skirmishers, they are highly vulnerable to swarms of light units, and won't last long under artillery fire. Unlike most other spiderbots, these have no resistance to EMP, making it vulnerable to spybots and paralyzer drones.
