## Description
The Gladiator is a mid-weight skirmisher tank equipped with a 3-shot-burst plasma cannon. With a solid mix of range, firepower, and mobility, it thrives in hit-and-run tactics, delivering volleys of damage before repositioning.

Gladiators also excel at dismantling T1 defense lines due to outranging everything up to heavy laser towers, and having the health to survive several direct hits.
