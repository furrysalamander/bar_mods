## Description
Legion's Advanced Metal Storage structure increases your metal storage capacity by 10,000.

It is generally not necessary to build these, since the usual solution for having too much metal in storage is simply to scale up more buildpower to make use of it, rather than bank it. The most common use would be to capture a lot of reclaim lying around the field, or in structures to be reclaimed.
