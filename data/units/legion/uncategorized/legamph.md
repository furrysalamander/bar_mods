## Description
The Telchine is a versatile amphibious assault bot engineered for shoreline control and underwater skirmishes. Equipped with a cleansing heat ray for ground targets and a depth-charge launcher for submerged threats, it is a natural predator in coastal zones and chokepoints. However, Telchines cannot fire while submerged! They can only use their anti-sub weapons from a coastline!

With solid armor and strong mobility, the Telchine excels at leading beachheads, pushing subs away from coastlines, or supporting amphibious pushes.
