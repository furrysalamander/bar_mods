## Description
The Hippotes is Legion's T1 heavy air transport, capable of picking up anything from a Commander, to T1 turrets, to heavier T2 units. It moves agonizingly slow, however, and is vulnerable to AA, even though it can take a few hits from missile turrets before going down thanks to its relatively generous HP.
