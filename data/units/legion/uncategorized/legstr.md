## Description
The Hoplite is a nimble raider bot engineered for speed and suppression. Outfitted with twin rapid-fire machine guns, it excels at harassing exposed units, pressuring undefended expansions, and catching slower targets off guard. Its guns possess some impulse factor, letting it push lighter units around.

Hoplites are powerful, but not meant to be sent to the front lines. Save them to punch through a vulnerable flank or reinforce your own failing one.
