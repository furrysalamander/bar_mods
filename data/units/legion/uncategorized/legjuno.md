## Description
The Juno can build and stockpile anti-micro-electronics missiles. These will overload the most sensitive electronics and cause them to explode. Ticks and other land scouts, most radar and jammer units and mines have these sensitive electronics. Air scouts are unaffected.

The missiles have to be launched manually, and the area of effect lasts around 25 seconds.

Some units with radar/jammer abilities have special Juno-protected electronics, such as Spy-bots.
