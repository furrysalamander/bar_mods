## Description
The Triton is a heavy, amphibious assault unit capable of seamlessly transitioning between land and sea combat. Armed with a powerful 600-range gauss cannon and thick armor plating, it excels at spearheading assaults across coastal lines and river crossings. It also packs a 450-range gatling gun, able to defend itself vs. light air and ground units. And, like most amphibious tanks, it takes only one-fifth the 'damage' from any EMP effects, making it difficult to stun.

Its unique mobility allows it to pressure enemy defenses in places where traditional tanks or ships fall short—whether rolling across beaches or surfacing in unexpected naval zones. Though not the fastest, its versatility and staying power make it a frontline powerhouse.
