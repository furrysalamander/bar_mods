## Description
Legion's Geothermal Powerplant produces a constant 300 energy when built on a steam vent. This provides a good springboard for further expansion, especially into Tier 2 units and infrastructure.

When destroyed or self-destructed, it deals massive damage in a large radius, so don't build anything valuable too close to it. Geothermal Powerplants are priority targets for your enemy!
