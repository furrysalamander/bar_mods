## Description
The Harbinger is a specialized minelayer bomber that deploys Light Mines in a straight line, turning entire corridors or choke points into deadly no-go zones. Designed to disrupt enemy advances and trap mobile forces, it excels at area control and passive zone denial. Best used on tight maps or in defensive preparations, the Harbinger can quietly shift the flow of battle with proper placement and timing.

It can produce 1 mine every 10s, costing 30 metal and 1620 energy per mine.
