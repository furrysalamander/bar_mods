## Description
The Medusa is a heavy rocket artillery tank built for overwhelming firepower and saturation bombardment. It marks enemies with its targeting laser, then fires a deadly volley of hexaburst homing rockets, blanketing enemy positions with artillery fire. It's ideal for softening up entrenched defenses before an assault.

While not stealthy like the Boreas, the Medusa trades subtlety for raw area denial. Its salvos can break formations, punish static build-ups, and force enemies to relocate. However, it requires vision support and protection due to its slow speed and lack of close-range defenses.
