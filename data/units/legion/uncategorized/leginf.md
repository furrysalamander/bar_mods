## Description
The Inferno is a long-range burst-fire artillery vehicle that rains fiery devastation across wide areas of the battlefield. Firing six napalm shells in a salvo, it blankets zones in lingering flames, disrupting enemy formations and softening defenses from afar.

Its napalm payload excels at area denial, making it ideal for locking down chokepoints, disrupting build operations, or flushing enemies out of cover. Though fragile up close and slow to reposition, it becomes a nightmare when left unchecked in the rear line.
