## Description
The Decurion is a durable frontline assault tank designed to punch through enemy lines with sustained firepower. It mows down lighter units with its gatling gun, and threatens other mid-weight units up close, though it rapidly loses accuracy at max range.

In early skirmishes, the Decurion can spearhead attacks or soak up fire while lighter Legion units deal damage from behind. Its high health pool makes it ideal for drawing turret fire and surviving longer in drawn-out engagements.
