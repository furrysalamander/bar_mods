## Description
The Aesacus is a dedicated torpedo bomber designed to hunt sea-based threats with precision and speed. Though its tracking torpedoes don't fire from as great a range as other factions' torpedo bombers, they instead fire in bursts. Its impressive airspeed and wide sight range make it ideal for patrol runs and opportunistic strikes along coastlines and deep water.
