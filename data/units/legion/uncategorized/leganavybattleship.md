## Description
The Scylla brings twin triple-burst plasma cannons with high power and AoE to the field, complete with the ability for the ship to invade inland regions by deploying legs and walking ashore, letting it strike from unexpected angles. It is cripplingly slow, however, and cannot dodge incoming fire, or even outrange most T2 land artillery, so use this ability with caution.
