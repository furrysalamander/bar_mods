# Energy Converter
> Converts 70 Energy into 1 Metal Per Second

**Faction:** Legion | **Category:** Buildings | **Tier:** T1

## Description
Tier 1 energy converters produce 1 metal out of 70 energy per second. They are useful for turning excess energy to a trickle of extra metal, though they are not quite as efficient as T2 converters.

Energy converters explode for 660 damage in 105 AoE when destroyed. Do not place them too close to other structures, as they may terribly damage your own base!
