## Description
The Glaucus is Legion's T1 scout/raider hovercraft. It moves quickly over land or water, making it perfect for attacking vulnerable enemy positions from unexpected angles. Weak on its own, it should not be expected to hold up in a straight fight.

Its heat ray weapon deals up to 50% less damage at the max extent of its range. It needs to be very close to get the most out of its weapon.
