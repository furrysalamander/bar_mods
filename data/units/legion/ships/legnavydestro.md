## Description
The Syracusia is a versatile Legion warship combining a brutal mid-range heatray with 2 autonomous strike drones. Its beam weapon shreds surface targets at destroyer range, while the onboard Drone Control Matrix deploys armed drones to extend its threat radius and sustain pressure on enemy fleets.

Its heat ray strikes for 410 damage every 2.4s in 36 AoE, able to hit other targets if closely-packed. Unlike other destroyers, it does require energy to fire.

Drones can acquire targets up to 1000 range away from the ship, and deal 36 damage at 300 range every 1.8s. Each has 415 health, and flies at 280.5 speed. They have 600 sight range. The Syracusia builds a new drone every 8s for 15 metal/500 energy.
