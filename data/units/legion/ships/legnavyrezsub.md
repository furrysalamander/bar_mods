## Description
The Dionysus is a stealthy utility unit that revives fallen Legion ships and reclaimable wrecks from beneath the waves. With strong reclaim and resurrection power plus solid mobility, it excels at turning naval battles into long-term value—quietly rebuilding your fleet from enemy losses.
