## Description
The Martyr is a compact, high-speed suicide drone. Once within 500 range of its target, it transforms into a missile designed to cripple tightly-grouped ground units or punch holes in static defenses. It has no 'attack', per se—its entire purpose is to close in and explode. While fragile, its low cost, fast build time, and high speed make it a potent tool when used in numbers or from unexpected angles.

Martyrs deal 440 damage in 200 AoE. However, they deal 25% less damage to Commanders. Beware of friendly fire!
