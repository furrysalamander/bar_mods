## Description
The Blindfold is a high-speed tactical bomber drone that specializes in electronic warfare. Instead of dealing damage directly, it disables or destroys enemy radar systems, mines, and stealth jammers using a small-scale Juno bomb—clearing the way for conventional forces to strike.

The Juno has high speed and surprisingly tanky frame for a bomber, making it able to deliver its payload into light AA so long as fighters aren't around. It's perfect for blinding enemy forces and softening up defenses before an assault.
