## Description
The Lance is a long-range skirmisher armed with a precision railgun that hits almost instantly, designed to chip away at enemies from a safe distance. With excellent vision and a reach that exceeds most T1 units, the Lance excels at softening up enemy forces before they can retaliate. Lance shots also have mild impulse factor, allowing them to push around lighter units. Remember to pair the Lance with spotter units to let it fire at max range.

Lance shots can overpenetrate targets as they fly, potentially hitting more than one unit! Line up your shots to maximize damage, but also beware of hitting your own units behind enemies. Also, beware of the Lance's limited cone of fire—it can only shoot targets within roughly 200 degrees in front of it.
