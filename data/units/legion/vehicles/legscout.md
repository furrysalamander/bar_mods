## Description
The Wheelie is a lightning-fast recon vehicle built for early map control and intelligence gathering. With unmatched speed and a powerful sensor suite, it can quickly scout expansions, identify enemy positions, and bait units into ambushes. While lightly armed with a burst machine gun, its true value lies in its speed and sight range. Use it to disrupt early expansions, force overreactions, or screen for heavier Legion forces — just don't expect it to survive a direct encounter.
