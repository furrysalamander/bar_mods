## Description
The Cetus is a light amphibious tank that can travel on land and underwater.

It possesses the dual advantages of being able to roll over rough terrain only bots could normally pass, and having a high-velocity cannon that can easily hit fast units. This gives it an edge vs. most quick units that would be sent to stop them, though they still falter vs. true tanks and static defenses.
