## Description
The Construction Vehicle can build basic T1 structures, and is an effective unit for expansion on relatively flat terrain.

It's quite slow going uphill. Escorting it with a few units when expanding is usually smart. Each constructor also increases the player's energy and metal storage capacity by 50.
