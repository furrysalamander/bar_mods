## Description
The Pawn is a basic infantry bot that's cheap to build, and can quickly devastate poorly defended expansions, artillery units, and even isolated commanders.

It melts in the face of heavier defenses and is especially vulnerable to riot tanks, but never underestimate its firepower—Pawns are one of the most cost-effective attackers in the whole game!
