## Description
The Lazarus is a resurrection bot that can turn wrecks into brand new units.

Being stealthy, means that Radar cannot spot them, so they can work safely as long as your enemies does not have line of sight on them. They are extremely fragile — use with caution!‍

_‍_It can also reclaim wrecks, rocks, and trees, optionally reclaim an entire area, for extra resources.
