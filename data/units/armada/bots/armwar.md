## Description
The Centurion is a durable heavy infantry bot with dual rapid fire lasers, which is perfect against swarms of light units. It has a lot of health for a Tech 1 unit and has the lowest energy per metal cost of all bots.

It can easily take out multiple light assault units like Grunts and Pawns, and can also outrange a Commander, so with a little micro you can force them to retreat.

They are easily outmaneuvered by rocket bots, plasma bots and medium tanks.
