## Description
The Tumbleweed is a crawling bomb designed for one purpose—to blow up on enemy units and structures. Very deadly vs. grouped-up units such as tanks, or anything with too short a range to kill them safely.

Crawling bombs do more damage and get an even larger Area of Effect if you self-destruct them. Thanks to their amphibious nature, they're also great for attacking and regaining sea territory.
