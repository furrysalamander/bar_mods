## Description
The Roughneck is a rapid-firing gunship. It has decent armor, able to withstand most T1 anti-air defenses. They excel at destroying vulnerable skirmishers and artillery, and focusing down tougher units. In significant numbers, they can easily threaten T3 units.

They are not effective at assaulting bases, due to their low speed. For this purpose, use bombers, or Hornets. It has heavier armor but weaker weapons than its Cortex counterpart, the Wasp. Roughnecks' speed and power gives them some use in naval warfare, as well, but beware true naval AA.
