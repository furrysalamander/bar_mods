## Description
Stiletto is an EMP bomber, which drops paralyzing charges to stun units in a long line. It's even hidden from radar, making it great for ambushes.

Coordinate attacks and defense with them to paralyze enemy units just before they engage. It is more effective than EMP drones, because they drop bombs before getting in AA range.

If you queue an attack order followed by a move, they can drop their EMP loads and immediately do a full turn. This way you can run away from enemy’s AA and supporting fighters.
