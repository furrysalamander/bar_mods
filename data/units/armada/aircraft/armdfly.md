## Description
Abductor is a fast, radar-stealthed transport aircraft armed with a powerful EMP beam. As a Heavy transport, it can take on board even the larger T2 units (i.e. Fatboy), and even airlift LLTs and other T1 towers with the Transportable (Heavy) feature. It also confers Stealth to its cargo, so Abductors can covertly drop forces into enemy territory while stunning dangerous units or defenses.

Abductors can "steal" enemy units by stunning them, then Loading them. Carried enemies can be automatically Reclaimed by Construction Turrets that have no other task at hand, and Commanders (and Decoys) can also Capture them.
