## Description
The Stormbringer is a light bomber designed to hit slow-moving targets, static structures, and execute bombing runs on enemy bases.

It is slightly weaker than its CORTEX counterpart, the Whirlwind. Always scout ahead to identify your targets and local anti-air, and send fighters ahead of your bombers to break through the enemy’s fighter air-wall.

It drops its bombs in a line, so set up attack patterns to hit enemies accordingly.
