## Description
The Oracle is an aircraft equipped with advanced radar and sonar. While in flight, it’s capable of scanning for units that are in the air, on land, and even underwater.

It has a good radar range and speed so it's the best unit you can use for scouting. Always send it ahead of your bombers to locate targets for your bombing runs.
