## Description
The Sabre is a light seaplane gunship with dual energy blasters. It can attack both land and sea units.

It has more health than the Light Gunship but also costs more to build, and is vulnerable to naval anti-air and fighter planes. They can plow straight through light missile turrets, but anything heavier is fatal.
