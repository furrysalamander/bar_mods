## Description
T2 Artillery can outrange most defenses and mobile units, while being fairly cheap to build. Mausers benefit from higher fire rate, a bit more range, and more accuracy than Quakers, but have drastically smaller explosion size and shot damage.

It moves very slowly and will be helpless alone, so be prepared to defend it with units or keep it behind your defensive line.
