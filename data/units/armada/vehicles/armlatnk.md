## Description
The Jaguar is a fast raider tank equipped with a lightning cannon (300 range) and a light anti-air turret. Lightning chains to nearby units, dealing light AoE damage.

It deals a good amount of damage in close quarters combat, but lacks armor to fight against heavier units like Tigers.
