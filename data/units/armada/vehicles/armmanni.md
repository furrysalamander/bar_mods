## Description
The Starlight is a mobile tachyon accelerator vehicle that delivers a devastating 2,500 burst damage with its beam, which can even pierce multiple enemies if it overkills one. Because it's a beam, though, its damage falls off up to 50% with range, becoming only 1,250 if firing at max range. It can fire every 4.7s.

It can take out most units, including Tech 3 units with enough hits, and can outrange most defensive structures.

However, it's quite costly and doesn't have much armor, and can only fire in a 180 degree frontal arc, making it defenseless when flanked. It also consumes 1,000 energy to fire each shot.
