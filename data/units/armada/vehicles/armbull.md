## Description
The Bull is a general purpose heavy assault tank equipped with a plasma cannon and significant armor. It is more expensive than its CORTEX counterpart, the Tiger, but also deals more damage with a larger area of effect (AoE) and more range. They excel at shielding more vulnerable units while dominating mid-range battles.

While sturdy and powerful, it loses out to the Tiger in staying power and all-round stats for cost. Consider making tank destroyers (Starlight) alongside them to deal with other tanks.
