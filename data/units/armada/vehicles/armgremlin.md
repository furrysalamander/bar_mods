## Description
Gremlin is a stealthy tank equipped with light plasma weapon. It doesn’t stand a chance facing other T2 units with it's weak armor and a low rate of fire.

Gremlins' cloak + stealth combo makes them impossible to spot without an Intrusion Countermeasure System. It will however decloak if it gets too close to an enemy.

Useless as a direct combat unit, it's far better as a cloaked scout and spotter for Mausers / Starlights.

Small groups can sabotage bases by sneaking in and targeting key buildings such as an Anti-Nuke or Fusion Reactor. Aim for volatile buildings to trigger chain reactions and cripple entire bases.

Without the enemy Commander using his D-gun, 4-6 gremlins will likely pull off a Commander kill.
