## Description
Shredder is a mobile anti-air tank equipped with a rapid-fire flak cannon. Its weapon explosion has a very large area of effect and deals ludicrous amounts of damage to aircraft, easily swatting T1 and most T2 aircraft that come into its vicinity.

It doesn’t have anything to fight with against land units, so keep it covered.
