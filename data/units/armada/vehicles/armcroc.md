## Description
The Turtle is an amphibious tank equipped with a cannon and heavy armor. It also packs a light AA missile launcher to defend itself from Shuriken in addition to 80% EMP resistance, making it difficult to stun. It has more health and range than its Cortex counterpart, the Salamander, but is also slower.

With slightly longer range and higher HP than even Bulls, as well as its amphibious capability granting it the same slope tolerance as bots, Turtles can handily defeat rocket bots and plasma, sweeping up most T1.

Unlike Bulls, they falter vs. heavy resistance. Turtles also have a short sight range and can't see as far as they can shoot, making them require a spotter or radar to use effectively.
