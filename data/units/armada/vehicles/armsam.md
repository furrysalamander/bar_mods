## Description
The Whistler is a medium-range missile truck that can outrange most Tier 1 defensive units, and doubles as light mobile AA. Its missiles track air, but NOT ground units. Its very large sight range and can easily spot targets for your forces.

It doesn't fare well in close-combat, so always keep it at a distance using the Fight command.
