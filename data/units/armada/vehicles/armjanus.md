## Description
The Janus fires twin medium-ranged missiles with each shot, which deal 660 maximum damage in a large radius. Its large single shot damage makes it a very deadly unit in the T1 stage. Use them to pack a powerful punch when dealing with high health units such as medium tanks and enemy Commanders.

The Janus has a long reload time, so engage the enemy with care and support them with other units.

A common tactic to counter Janus is luring their heat-seeking missiles into the midst of allied formations - another good reason to micromanage your Janus regiment.

The Janus cannot see as far as it can shoot, so it's important to pair it with high-vision units or Radar Towers. Its missiles also cannot track air units, making it vulnerable to Shurikens.
