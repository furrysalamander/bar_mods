## Description
The Blitz is a fast assault tank with dual rapid-fire cannons for close combat engagements. For a low cost, they offer excellent speed and DPS.

Packs of Blitz are excellent for raiding bases and chasing down enemy raiders. Their speed is an important advantage, especially on large and flat maps.

Blitz are great for raiding metal extractors and other economic structures. Engaging enemy light units is also viable - flank them from multiple angles to benefit from flanking damage.

The Blitz lacks armor and will melt in the face of heavy combat. They are especially vulnerable to Pounders.
