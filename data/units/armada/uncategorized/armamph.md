## Description
The Platypus is a very fast amphibious bot equipped with a light laser and a basic anti-air missile launcher.

It is unique among all amphibious units for floating on the water's surface instead of walking on the seafloor, allowing it to fire its anti-air weapons on water, unlike Archangels.

Highly expensive for a raider, it's best built in small raiding parties, or to supplement other landing forces vs. fast units and light aircraft—especially EMP drones.
