## Description
The Fatboy is a heavy mid-range plasma bot that deals ludicrous amounts of damage to units within its area of effect (AoE).

It moves very slowly and will be helpless alone, so be prepared to defend it with units or keep it behind your defensive line.

Fatboys have synergy with Sharpshooters when it comes to facing Sheldons, a mainstay of Cortex T2 Bots. If Sheldons advance to attack the sniper bots, they must enter Fatboy range, opening themselves up to devastating AoE fire.
