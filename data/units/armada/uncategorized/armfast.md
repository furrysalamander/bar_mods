## Description
The Sprinter is a fast raider bot that's equipped with a short-range rapid-fire machine gun.

In a stand-up fight, Sprinters lose badly. Use their speed and power to break through gaps in defensive lines, outrunning enemy pursuit and striking vulnerable targets. They are FAR too expensive to spam—use Pawns instead if you need light infantry.

Use their speed to surround enemy units and buildings, gaining flanking damage bonuses to kill them much faster.
