## Description
The Archangel is an advanced anti-air bot equipped with both long-range (1200) and mid-range (880) rapid-fire missiles.

It's amphibious and deals a significant amount of damage to most aircraft, but can't shoot while underwater. Still, nothing beats it for long-range mobile defense against gunships & bombers.
