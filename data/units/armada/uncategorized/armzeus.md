## Description
The Welder is a general-purpose assault bot equipped with a lightning cannon. Welders form the heavy infantry core of Armada's bot forces, optimized for killing fast units that would threaten their backline.

Welders require energy to fire, so keep an eye on your energy supply!

It's quite slow and has short range, but is very good at defending weaker units like Hounds and Sharpshooters.
