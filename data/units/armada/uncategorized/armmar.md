## Description
The Marauder is a fast amphibious assault mech that is equipped with two powerful gauss guns and twin light anti-air missile turrets.

Its high speed and amphibious capabilities make it a natural choice for fast and devastating raids on the enemy.

Don't be afraid to engage in direct combat against lighter units and defenses. The deadly combo of high HP and speed enables a well-choreographed Marauder raid to cut straight through anything fast enough to intercept them.

Defend against Marauders by mining areas susceptible to Marauder raids. A rapid and powerful air response is another way to deal with them.
