## Description
The Razorback is a fast experimental battle mech armed with two powerful rapid-fire laser guns. It rips enemies apart, even accounting for laser damage falloff with range. It can also accurately target and shoot down air units, making it dangerous to engage with gunships.

Its brutal firepower and speed make it ideal vs. most units, though its pool of health is not that large. It can be countered by long-range units like the Sharpshooter, Starlight and Pulsar.
