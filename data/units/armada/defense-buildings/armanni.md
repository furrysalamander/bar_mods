## Description
The Pulsar is a long-range tachyon accelerator laser tower that delivers a devastating amount of damage. It is equipped to deal with the heaviest enemy units in the field.

It can take out most units, including Tech 3 units with enough hits, and is high enough to shoot over other defensive structures. However, each shot consumes a massive amount of energy, so keep an eye on your stores.

Pulsars have trouble when targeting radar dots due to being precision weapons, so the team building them should make targeting facilities to increase accuracy.
