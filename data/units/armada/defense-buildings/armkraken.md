## Description
The Gorgon is a floating rapid-fire plasma tower that can be built on the water. It outranges anything up to Destroyers, and is highly effective vs. any close-ranged combat ships and hovercraft. It remains vulnerable to subs and long-range ships. Still, its relatively low cost makes it valuable, especially for defending subs and submerged installations from surface vessels.
