## Description
The Manta is a floating heavy laser tower that can be built on water, and is notably sturdier than its land version. It excels vs. corvettes, frigates and hovertanks.

Despite its long range and heavy damage, it is hopeless when built alone, and is useless vs. subs. So remember to pair it with other defensive structures.
