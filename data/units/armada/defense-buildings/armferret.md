## Description
The Ferret is an anti-air missile battery that can easily shoot down most T1 and even some T2 aircraft.

It has more health than the Nettle—especially when popped down. Somewhat more reliable vs. bombers and gunships, it still should not be relied on as a primary defense due to its cost, though its ambush capability gives it unique value in some situations.
