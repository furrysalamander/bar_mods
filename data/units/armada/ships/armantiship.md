## Description
Support Ships fulfill 3 roles: intel; mobile energy generation; and anti-nuke capability on water. They are the only source of nuke protection for your fleets, so ensure you have at least one.

Each ship makes roughly a third as much as a Fusion Reactor at almost a third of the cost. This allows you to take a good chunk of your energy infrastructure with your fleet.
