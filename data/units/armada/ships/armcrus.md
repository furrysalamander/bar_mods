## Description
Paladins are medium assault cruisers. They are like an upgraded version of Destroyers, and are a casual part of a T2 naval strike force. Equipped with a medium-range plasma cannon, two light laser turrets and a depth charge launcher, they are very versatile.

Quite fast among T2 ships, they are useful for rushing your enemy at the beginning of the T2 sea battle stage, and remain a fleet mainstay thereafter. They can easily take out any T1 units, and offer very good protection against hovercraft in particular.
