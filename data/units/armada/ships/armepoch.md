## Description
The Epoch is Armada's Flagship—a massive artillery platform combining overwhelming firepower with unmatched range. Once you have one, it is usually game over for the enemy.

They are equipped with five plasma cannon turrets, with the two main cannons having the longest range. On most maps, it can bombard any point on land, if you manage to bring it close to the shore.

The other three cannons (830 range) are used to kill off anything that manages to get closer. Thanks to their power, range and speed, they can handily destroy cruisers and other ships.

They are also equipped with two heavy anti-air flak cannons (775 range). However, they can still be vulnerable to torpedo bombers, and especially battle submarines, so never leave them without an escort.
