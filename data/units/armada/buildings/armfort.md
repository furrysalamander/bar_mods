## Description
The Fortification Wall is an advanced defensive wall that blocks most Tech 1 & Tech 2 units and low-altitude projectiles. Advanced walls have almost four times the health of a basic Dragon’s Teeth, and will take quite a beating before they break.

They can also be used to help protect Beamers, Overwatch, and especially Pulsar towers, all of which can fire over them.

High-trajectory artillery can also safely shoot from behind walls.

Walls resist crushing by heavy T2 units, but some massive T3 will plow right through them.
