## Description
The Mangonel is a light hovercraft with a rocket launcher. Good for taking out defensive structures and softening up an enemy expansion before you push in. As it launches vertically, it can fire over walls and most terrain. Its extreme damage per shot (for T1) often overkills units so much that they leave no wreck, denying reclaim to your enemy.

It does slightly more damage than its ARMADA counterpart, the Possum, and its range is among the highest within Tech 1 units.
