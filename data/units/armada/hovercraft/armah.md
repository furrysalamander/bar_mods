## Description
The Sweeper is a mobile anti-air hovercraft that's perfect for taking out light aircraft over the sea and land, while enjoying immunity to torpedoes. It even has built-in low-range sonar!

It's quite expensive and not the strongest for its role, though, so try not to build more than you need.
