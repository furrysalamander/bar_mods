## Description
Fury is a mobile anti-air tank equipped with a rapid-fire flak cannon. Its weapon deals ludicrous amounts of AoE damage to aircraft, easily swatting T1 and most T2 aircraft that come into its vicinity.

It doesn’t have anything to fight with against land units, so keep it covered.
