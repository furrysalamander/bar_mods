## Description
‍Quakers are heavy mobile artillery vehicles with an emphasis on breaking apart enemy formations. Though they fire slower than Mausers at 5s reload, they deal 420 damage in 72 AoE, making them more reliable vs. groups.

It moves very slowly and will be helpless alone, so be prepared to defend it with units or keep it behind your defensive line.
