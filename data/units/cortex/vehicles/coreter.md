## Description
Obscurer is a mobile radar-jamming vehicle that can hide your units from enemy radar. They are very useful to limit the enemy's capability to strike beyond visual range. Do note they have a high energy cost and constantly consume energy to maintain the jamming field.

Be aware even a single hit will temporarily disable jamming ability.
