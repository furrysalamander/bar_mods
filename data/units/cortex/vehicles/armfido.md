## Description
The Hound is a fast and decently tough bot with a mid-range, low-velocity plasma mortar. Their firing range is longer than their sight, so provide spotters like a radar bot.

Hounds lose out in raw damage output compared to the Recluse, but are faster and cheaper. They're a cost-effective option for cleaning up T1 units, as not much in T1 can compete with them.
