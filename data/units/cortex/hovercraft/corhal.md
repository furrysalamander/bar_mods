## Description
The Halberd is a remarkably heavyweight unit for T1, boasting armor and firepower on par with T2 frontline tanks.

However, each shot costs 75 energy, or about 93/s firing continuously.

Its low maneuverability and range limits it to a heavy assault/escort role, similar to the Sumo. It can reliably kill Dolphins in 4 shots, and assault naval/coastal bases while enjoying hovercrafts' torpedo immunity.

Combine with Mangonels to soften up defenses for a deadly T1 pushing force.
