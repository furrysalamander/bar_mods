## Description
The Devastator is a floating plasma and beam laser turret platform that can be built on the water. Its multi-layered weaponry lays waste to surface vessels and hovercraft, forming a focal defense point against naval attack.

The main gun fires 420 damage rounds with 164 AoE every 2.1s. It also has a heavy laser at 630 range, and twin 370 range lasers. All of its guns are capable of tracking targets independently. However, it is helpless vs. air attack.
