## Description
The Cerberus is a triple-barreled artillery platform firing salvos of three shells at a time, effectively suppressing enemy formations. It also acts as a Geothermal Powerplant that produces 450 energy.

Cerberus is good for locking down a geothermal vent in a risky position, such as one close to the frontline. Save safe vent spots for T2 geos.
