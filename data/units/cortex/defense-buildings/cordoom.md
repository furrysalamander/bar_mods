## Description
The Bulwark is a sturdy multi-weapon tower with three laser cannons of short to long range, allowing it to attack almost any target but truly long-range artillery. The main gun deals 4,500 damage every 6s, and its green and red lasers (247 and 40 damage respectively) can track enemies independently, letting it focus its power on dangerous foes. The Bulwark's generous damage and thick armor makes it an effective focal point against enemy pushes, but make sure to keep it well supported.

When threatened, the Bulwark can be turned to the OFF state, causing it to retract its weapons and bunker down into its armored shell, nearly quadrupling its effective HP. This helps it survive concentrated fire until it can be repaired.

The Bulwark requires considerable amounts of energy to fire. Keep an eye on your stores.
