## Description
The Basilisk is an LRPC (Long-Range Plasma Cannon) that can shoot targets across the map. Its shells are devastating vs. formations of tightly-packed units (157 AoE), and inflict more damage overall than their Armada counterpart. However, they also fire slower (16s), and require slightly more space to build.

It becomes a priority target when built, so make sure you keep it safe. It can also be countered by Overseers, but at a high energy cost to your enemy.

LRPCs also make good naval gun batteries, outranging any ship or amphibious unit. They're an asset for reclaiming sea territory.
