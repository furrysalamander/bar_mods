## Description
The Catalyst is a tactical missile launcher that obliterates most units with its 4,000 damage and massive 380 blast radius. Each missile must be built separately, for a cost in time and resources. The Catalyst can store up to 10 missiles, and fire them in quick succession (2s reload time).

They’re great for rendering enemy defenses useless before an assault, and for destroying advancing units while you build a quick reaction force to combat them. Catalyst missiles can easily wipe out entire groups of enemies in a single shot. They can also be effective on enemy fleets idling offshore—including submarines and amphibious forces, as the blast is large enough to reach beneath the water.
