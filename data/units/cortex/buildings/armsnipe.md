## Description
The Sharpshooter is a long-range sniper bot that deals 2500 damage per shot. Their shots hit instantly, and often overkill enemies so much they leave no reclaimable wreck.

It takes 10 seconds to reload, uses 500 energy per shot, and its ability to cloak lets you move it into place for the perfect shot and re-cloak afterwards.
