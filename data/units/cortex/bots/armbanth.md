## Description
The Titan takes the title of the heaviest ARMADA unit with stride. Packing an anti-heavy tachyon accelerator, heat seeking vertical-launch missiles, close-quarters blasters with considerable AOE and a massive health reserve, the Titan is designed to deal with any (non-aerial or submerged) threat.

Titans can go anywhere to defend and attack from every angle. Park them on the front to reinforce against enemy attacks or send them across uneven terrain to strike the enemy where vehicles can't.

The Titan is specifically good in dealing with smaller sized armies, and shines most with help from supporting units. Vision from allied scouts like Ticks and fire support from ranged units like the Sniper and Starlight can make all the difference when engaging with Titans.
