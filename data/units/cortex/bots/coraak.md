## Description
The Manticore is an advanced anti-air bot equipped with long-range (1300) missiles and a medium-range (775) flak cannon. The missiles fire every 1.60s, while the flak only every 5.50s.

It's amphibious and deals a significant amount of damage to most aircraft, but can't shoot while underwater. Still, nothing beats it for long-range mobile anti-air defense.
