## Description
The Catapult is an experimental heavy rocket bot that fires a barrage of unguided heavy missiles in a spread pattern around the target. It has a long reload time.

It is extremely effective against grouped units, and can clear out defensive structures. It's also good for attacking enemy fleets lingering too close to shore, allowing you room to push back into the water.
