## Description
The Mammoth is a heavily-armored assault bot equipped with a heavy beam laser, dealing up to 302 damage every 0.6s. Its range makes it brutal at mid-range skirmishing, but also capable of dealing with both light and heavy units alike. Its main drawbacks are its pitifully slow speed, and 75 energy drain per shot.

It has the most health among all Cortex Tech 2 land units, and is the best choice for tanking enemy fire. When you have enough of them, they become quite an unstoppable force.
