## Description
The Bedbug is a crawling bomb designed for one purpose—to blow up on enemy units and structures. Very deadly vs. grouped-up units such as tanks, or anything lacking the range to deal with them safely.

Crawling bombs will do more damage if you self-destruct them. Thanks to their amphibious nature, they're also great for attacking and regaining sea territory.
