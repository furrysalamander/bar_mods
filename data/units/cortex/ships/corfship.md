## Description
Raider ships give T2 Cortex fleets a fast and fierce attacker, complete with light AA for fending off drones and seaplanes. While almost as expensive as Destroyers, their speed, flamethrowers (450 range) and decent armor make for a highly effective strike force.

However, their only defense vs. submarines is to outrun them, and T2 adv. torpedo launchers that home in on targets nullify their speed advantage. They also struggle to defeat Cruisers, a mainstay of T2 fleets.
