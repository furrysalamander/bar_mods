## Description
Predator is a submarine killer. It is ideal for obliterating enemy submarines, in particular the T2 battle submarines. Predators move more swiftly, turn faster and have a better angle of fire compared to other submarines. These attributes give the Predator unmatched lethality against battle submarines which against an unguarded fleet would cause devastation. Always include a few of these in your T2 navy.
