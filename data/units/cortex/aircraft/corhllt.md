## Description
The Twin Guard is a dual light laser turret ("DLLT") that can take out light vehicles and bots. It's literally two Guards stacked atop each other, with a bit more armor. Each gun tracks and fires independently, so it can attack two targets at once.

It is an excellent choice to combat unit swarms, and its high DPS and range makes it a formidable threat to enemy commanders.

Note: Only the topmost laser has the range to attack rocket bots at max distance.
