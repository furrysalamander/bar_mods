## Description
Hailstorm is a heavy bomber that can deal great amounts of damage.

It deals much higher damage than its T1 form (Whirlwind). As little as 3 Hailstorms can destroy a fusion powerplant on one flyby (4-5 for an adv. fusion). They can drop another round of bombs every 7 seconds. It is more expensive but also stronger than its Armada counterpart.
