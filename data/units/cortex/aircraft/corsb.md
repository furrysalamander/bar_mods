## Description
The Dam Buster is a seaplane bomber with a payload of 12 bombs. It’s designed to hit slow-moving ships and static naval structures, and execute unanticipated bombing runs on land bases.

Dam Busters are unique among bombers for their bombs' ability to bounce across the surface of water without exploding, potentially hitting enemy units forward of the impact site. This allows them to potentially fire from an extended range.
