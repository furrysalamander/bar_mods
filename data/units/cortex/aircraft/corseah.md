## Description
Skyhook is an armored transportation aircraft. It can take on board even the heavier T2 units and easily withstand light anti-air defenses. As a Heavy transport, it can also airlift LLTs, and other T1 towers with the Transportable (Heavy) feature.

It's slower, but way more armored than its Armada counterpart.
